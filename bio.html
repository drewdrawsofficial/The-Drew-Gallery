Artist statement

My work is an extension of my soul. I never really feel as if my voice is heard unless its in art form. Being one of the few art kids in the city of Mount Vernon i often felt alone. Art is its own language, not everyone speaks it but its never too late to learn. May it be a drawing, painting or photo it will always feel right if it authentically me. Nothing on this sight was made for the grade alone but for me to be able to speak with no words, but images.
